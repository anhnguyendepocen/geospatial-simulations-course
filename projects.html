Projects

Throughout the semester, the students develop and implement a geospatial simulation tool using one of the techniques covered by the course with application in their study scientific domain. The development will be performed incrementally over the course of the semester. First, students will identify a research question that requires simulation of geospatial process or phenomena and exploration of system behavior under a selected set of conditions. Second, students will be required to develop a methodological workflow and identify the appropriate programming tools for implementation. Finally, the project culminates with a research presentation delivered to the rest of the class at the end of the semester and a short paper. Documentation of the simulation tool and a live demonstration will be required. The final design will be judged on methodological rigor, creativity, and presentation.
